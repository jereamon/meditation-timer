I've been meditating for a while now and have begun to anticipate when my timer will go off. The amount of time I meditate fits into my schedule well but I wanted something to set my timer a bit more randomly ensuring that I'll be unable to predict its end.

After clicking begin select how many minutes you want your timer to run for. Then, optionally select one of two radio buttons. Either radio button will cause your timer to run for a random amount of time. One will be between 90% and 110% of your selected time. The other will be 90% to 100%.
