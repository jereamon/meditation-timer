I've been meditating for a while now and have begun to anticipate when my timer will go off. The amount of time I meditate fits into my schedule well but I wanted something to set my timer a bit more randomly so that I won't know when it will go off.

After clicking begin select how many minutes you want your timer to run for. Then, optionally select one of two radio buttons. Either radio button will cause your timer to run for a random amount of time. One will be between 10% longer or shorter than your selected time. The other will only be up to 10% longer.
